Digital Carbon Footprint Calculator


Welcome to the digital carbon footprint calculator from Green Path! This allows you to calculate how much of an environmental impact your tablet, laptop, and computer use will have. You can obtain an understanding of the carbon emissions linked to your digital activities by entering data about how you use your devices. With the help of this calculator, we hope you will consider how you use technology and be motivated to change to more environmentally friendly behaviours. Let's work together to create a more environmentally conscious future by bringing attention to the negative effects of our digital lifestyles on the environment. Participate in the shift by beginning to calculate now!
